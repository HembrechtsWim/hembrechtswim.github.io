Who am I?

Hello my name is Wim Hembrechts. In 2020 I started my study career in applied sciences at the IT Factory Thomas More in Geel. I've always been interested in computers and everything that goes with them. That's why I chose this course. During the first year I participated in two project and it turned out that the cloud and cybersecurity project appealed to me the most. That's the reason I continued in this study path. Although I am happy in my current field of study and can see myself working in this sector, I still have no idea what kind of job I want to do after this study. Mainly because the IT security sector is very big and full of variety.

Of course I'm not only interested in IT, in my high school career I studied industrial engineering. Afterwards I also studied the same at KU Leuven for two years before I decided that this wasn't quite what I wanted. You can find out more things about me and my career in my resume.
